Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Etsy Content Scheduler application ("the App") handles your information when you use our content scheduling service for Pinterest.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects and processes the following information:

  • Pinterest Account Information: Your Pinterest username and profile information through OAuth authentication
  • Product Data: Images, titles, descriptions, and links that you choose to schedule for posting
  • Scheduling Data: Information about when you want your content to be published
  • Usage Data: Information about which pins have been posted and when

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your Pinterest account
  • Create and schedule pins on your behalf as instructed by you
  • Track posting history to prevent duplicate posts
  • Provide you with scheduling and analytics features

4. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not maintain any centralized servers or databases
  • Pinterest authentication tokens are stored securely on your local machine
  • Posting history is maintained locally to prevent duplicates

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your information to any third parties. The only data transmission occurs between:

  • Your device and Pinterest's API (to post your content)
  • Your device and Etsy (when accessing your product information)

6. Pinterest API Compliance

This App uses the Pinterest API and complies with Pinterest's:

  • Developer and API Terms of Service
  • Developer Guidelines
  • Community Guidelines

We access your Pinterest account only with your explicit authorization through OAuth 2.0.

7. User Control

You have full control over your data:

  • You manually select each product to be posted
  • You review and approve all content before scheduling
  • You can delete local data at any time
  • You can revoke API access through your Pinterest account settings

8. Data Retention

  • Posting history is retained locally until you delete it
  • No data is retained after you uninstall the application
  • Authentication tokens expire and must be renewed periodically

9. Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Local encryption of sensitive credentials
  • No transmission of passwords or login credentials
  • Regular security updates

10. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

12. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored by the application
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Revoke API access through Pinterest
  • Request information about how your data is used

14. Compliance

This application complies with:

  • Pinterest Developer Guidelines
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) where applicable
