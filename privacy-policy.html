1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Vlad Bidilean, operating as PurrfectPlace ("we", "us", "our"), handles your information when you use the Etsy Content Scheduler application for Pinterest content scheduling.

2. Information We Collect via Pinterest API

PurrfectPlace collects and processes the following information through the Pinterest API:

  • Pinterest Account Information: Your Pinterest username, profile information, and user ID obtained through OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Board Information: Names and IDs of your Pinterest boards for pin placement
  • Pin Content: Images, titles, descriptions, and links that you choose to schedule for posting
  • Scheduling Data: Information about when you want your content to be published
  • Posting History: Records of which pins have been posted and when
  • Analytics Data: Basic performance metrics (saves, clicks, impressions) for posted pins

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected from Pinterest solely for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your Pinterest account through OAuth 2.0
  • Automated Posting: To create and publish pins on your behalf at scheduled times as you instruct
  • Posting Management: To track posting history and prevent duplicate posts
  • Scheduling: To maintain your posting schedule and queue
  • Analytics: To provide you with basic performance insights for your pins
  • Service Improvement: To improve the scheduling features and user experience

4. Data Sharing and Selling

We do not share, sell, or distribute your Pinterest data to any third parties. Your information is used exclusively for the scheduling service you requested.

The only data transmission occurs between:

  • Your device and Pinterest's API (to authenticate and post your content)
  • Your device and Etsy's website (when you select products to share)

We may disclose information only if required by law, legal process, or government request.

5. Data Storage and Security

Storage:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not maintain any centralized servers or cloud databases
  • Pinterest authentication tokens are stored securely on your local machine
  • Posting history is maintained locally to prevent duplicates
  • Data is stored only as long as needed to provide the scheduling service

Security:

  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data
  • Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication (industry standard)
  • Local encryption of sensitive credentials and tokens
  • No transmission or storage of passwords or login credentials
  • Regular security updates to the application

6. Pinterest API Compliance

PurrfectPlace uses the Pinterest API and fully complies with:

  • Pinterest Developer and API Terms of Service
  • Pinterest Developer Guidelines
  • Pinterest Community Guidelines

We access your Pinterest account only with your explicit authorization through OAuth 2.0.

7. User Control

You have full control over your data:

  • You manually select each product and pin to be posted
  • You review and approve all pin content before scheduling
  • You can delete local data at any time
  • You can revoke API access through your Pinterest account settings at any time
  • You can contact us to request information about your data

8. Data Retention

  • Posting history is retained locally until you delete it
  • No data is retained after you uninstall the application
  • Pinterest authentication tokens expire and must be renewed periodically
  • You can request deletion of any stored data by contacting us

9. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data is stored
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Revoke: Revoke API access through Pinterest account settings
  • Contact: Contact us with questions about how your data is used
  • Update: Request corrections to any stored information

10. Children's Privacy
